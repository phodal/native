fix: **Idle disabled buttons no longer wear an accent outline**: `primary`/`destructive` buttons kept their full-strength accent border while disabled, outlining the muted disabled fill in vivid accent — which read as a permanent focus ring on every idle disabled button (a comment composer's empty-draft send button, most visibly). Disabled filled buttons now mute their border with their fill; ghost keeps its borderless shape, and explicit `style.border` overrides still win.
- **Programmatic focus is quiet on non-editables**: source-driven `autofocus` and the automation `focus`/`select` actions granted `focus_visible` to any widget, so a window-level default focus landing on a button dressed it in the ring-offset focus ring at rest. Programmatic focus now follows the pointer contract — buttons and rows take focus quietly, editable text kinds show ring and caret however focus arrives, and keyboard focus (Tab, arrows) stays loud.
