feature: **Button fidelity round**: the button family (`button`, `icon_button`, `toggle_button`, `toggle`) now carries the full house register. Metrics: heights land on the whole-pixel 32/36/40 ladder (`sm`/default/`lg`; `icon` stays the default square) instead of the multiplicative scale's 31.5/40.5; side insets breathe at 12/16/24 and pull in one step (10/12/16) when an inline icon already weights the edge; the corner radius and the 14px label hold ONE step across sizes — small buttons stop reading as chips with caption text, large ones as cards; the lg icon gap stays at 8 (a bigger button earns air at its edges, not between icon and label). Labels draw with the medium companion face via the new `typography.button_font_id` token (null resolves to the built-in medium id while the house sans is in play, follows a theme's custom `font_id` otherwise; hosts without a mapped medium face keep regular outlines at the same measured advances). States: filled variants press one wash step past hover (80%/90% alpha over one base color, scheme-free); quiet variants press to the deeper neutral wash while a toggle's on-state keeps the muted wash and selected NAV buttons keep bare chrome; disabled fades fill, border, and ink to half strength — the same wash the selection controls wear — so a disabled ghost no longer grows a gray box and a disabled primary still reads as a primary. Filled buttons settle onto the page with a new whisper shadow step (`shadow.xs`, 1px drop / 2px blur at half the shadow ink; quiet variants, toggles, and disabled buttons stay flat, and a translucent themed fill casts none).
