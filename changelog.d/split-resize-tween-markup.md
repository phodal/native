feature: **Animated split resize in markup**: `resize-duration` (milliseconds) and `resize-easing` (`linear`/`standard`/`emphasized`/`spring`) on `split` turn the bound `value` into a target — a model-driven move eases the rendered fraction there one presented frame at a time on the recorded frame clock instead of snapping, dispatching the same `on-resize` echoes a drag would; reduced-motion appearances snap automatically, both markup engines and the Zig builder (`ElementOptions.resize_duration`/`resize_easing`) lower to the same runtime layout tween, and the validator teaches the split-only scope, the closed easing vocabulary, and easing-without-duration.
