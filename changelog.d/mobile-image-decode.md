feature: **Real cover art on phones — the embed image-decode service**: the mobile hosts gain the platform image codec through a new embed seam (`native_sdk_app_set_image_service`), mirroring the audio service pattern — the iOS toolkit host registers CGImageSource (ImageIO, the same codec family the macOS host decodes through) and the Android toolkit host registers BitmapFactory over JNI (decoding straight-alpha RGBA8 directly into the runtime's decode buffer through a direct ByteBuffer, no extra pixel copy) — so `fx.registerImageBytes` decodes PNG/JPEG for real on both platforms and the soundboard's committed album covers render on the simulator and emulator instead of the initials fallback. Decoding runs once per registration (frames reference the runtime's bounded image registry afterwards), a shim answer with dimensions the buffer cannot hold is refused before any pixel slice forms, and embed hosts without a registered codec keep the honest decline: `error.UnsupportedService`, image/avatar widgets on their fallback.
