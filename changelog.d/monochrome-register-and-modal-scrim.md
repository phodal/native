improvement: **Monochrome primary register**: the default theme's primary is now the register's near-black (light) / porcelain-white (dark) monochrome pair instead of the blue-violet, with high-contrast accents and focus rings re-derived to the same monochrome extremes; the showcase themes (notes, calculator, soundboard, system-monitor, markdown-viewer) re-derive their neutrals onto the register's neutral/stone/zinc scale anchors so each app keeps its accent personality on the same gray foundation.
- **Modal scrim with real backdrop blur**: dialogs, drawers, and sheets now paint a token-driven scrim behind themselves — a genuine backdrop blur of the already-painted content (`blur.scrim`, default 4) plus a translucent wash (`colors.scrim`, default 10% black) across the whole surface; anchored surfaces (popover, menus, tooltip) never scrim, `Widget.scrim = false` embeds a modal surface as an inline preview, and high-contrast themes trade the glass for a decisive dim.
