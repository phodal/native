feature: **The Geist pack's button group renders as detached chip triggers**: a new `button_group_style` register token (`.segmented` house bar or `.detached` chips) with a `button_group` control table and a `button_group_gap` metric. Under the pack, every group member is its own fully-rounded borderless chip 8px from its neighbor — the rest chip on a translucent gray wash (black 8% light, white 9% dark) under the primary ink, the selected chip ink-inverted (gray-1000 fill under page-color knockout, deliberately one step short of the pack's pure-black light primary), disabled the gray-200/gray-900 swap pair. Exclusive-choice behavior, member handlers, and focus are untouched — styling only, and an author-stated gap still wins over the metric. The house segmented register is byte-identical (catalog and preview signatures unmoved, zero docs preview churn), and the new register is pinned per scheme alongside a house pin of the same specimen. The `ControlVisualTokens` table grew an `active_foreground` channel for ink-inverted active fills, available to every theme through the override tier.
